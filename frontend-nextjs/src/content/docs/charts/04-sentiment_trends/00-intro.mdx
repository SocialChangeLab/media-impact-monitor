<p className="lead">The Sentiment Trend Charts in the Media Impact Monitor dashboard visually represent the media coverage sentiment towards protests and policy within a selected time frame. 
This tool aids NGOs in understanding how their protests are perceived in the media, helping them gauge their actions' effectiveness and public impact.</p>
