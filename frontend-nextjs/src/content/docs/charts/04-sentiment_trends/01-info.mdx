The **Sentiment Trend Chart** visualizes the media's sentiment regarding the protests and progressive climate policies.

## In the chart

- **Horizontal X-Axis:** Daily, weekly, or monthly timeline over which articles where published.
- **Vertical Y-Axis:** Number of articles
- **Colour of Lines:** The colours correspond to positive, neutral, or negative sentiment (see the Legend below the chart)

## Interaction

- ✦ **Hover** over the chart to see the exact frequencies. 
- ✲ **Compute impacts** by pressing the button below the chart.
