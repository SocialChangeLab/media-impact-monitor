title: How to read this chart?
date: 2024-06-26
---

The **Topics Trend Chart** shows how often common topics in the public discourse were mentioned.

## In the chart

- **Horizontal X-Axis:** Daily, weekly, or monthly timeline over which articles where published.
- **Vertical Y-Axis:** Number of articles mentioning a topic. 
- **Colour of Lines:** The colours correspond to the respective topics (see the Legend below the chart)

## Interaction

- ✦ **Hover** over the chart to see the exact frequencies. 
- ✲ **Compute impacts** by pressing the button below the chart.
