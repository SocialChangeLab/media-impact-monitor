Most of the data for our **Protest Timeline Chart** comes from the [Armed Conflict Location & Event Data Project (ACLED)](https://acleddata.com/), which compiles protests all over the world and makes them publicly accessible.

Additionally, we are complementing the ACLED dataset with data from some of the organisations' press releases on their websites (e.g., Last Generation) to ensure we capture as many protests as possible.

## Data limitations

- Sometimes, ACLED can miss a protest
